🇰🇪 Citizen Petition for Accountability

This is a peaceful, citizen-led petition expressing public concern about governance, economic hardship, taxation pressure, and leadership accountability in Kenya.

Why Citizens Are Signing

  • High cost of living
  • Economic hardship
  • Taxation concerns
  • Youth unemployment
  • Governance accountability concerns
  • Public dissatisfaction with leadership direction

Public Statement

We, the undersigned citizens, express concern about national leadership and call for urgent accountability, reform, and consideration of resignation in the interest of national stability and economic recovery.
